# Research Planning Prompt

Use this when you want an AI tool to turn a rough idea into a tractable thesis task.

## Prompt

```text
Act as a research planning assistant.
Turn my current thesis idea into a concrete work package.
Give me:
1. the smallest useful milestone,
2. the assumptions I am making,
3. the main risks or unknowns,
4. a 3-step plan for today,
5. what I should read or verify before drafting prose.

Context:
[PASTE CONTEXT HERE]
```
