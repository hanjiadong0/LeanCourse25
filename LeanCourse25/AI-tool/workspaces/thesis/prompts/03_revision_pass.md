# Revision Pass Prompt

Use this after you already have a draft.

## Prompt

```text
Revise the following thesis draft for clarity, structure, and academic tone.
Do not add fake facts or citations.
Mark places where the logic jumps, where evidence is missing, or where wording is too vague.

Return:
- a revised version,
- a short list of high-risk claims,
- a final checklist before submission.

Draft:
[PASTE DRAFT]
```
