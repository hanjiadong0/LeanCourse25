# Chapter Draft Prompt

Use this when you already know the content and want help drafting a section without losing the research logic.

## Prompt

```text
Help me draft a thesis section.
Do not invent citations.
Keep the structure academically conservative and explicit.

I need:
- a short outline,
- a clean first draft,
- a list of claims that still need citation or evidence,
- a revision checklist for clarity and rigor.

Section topic:
[PASTE TOPIC]

Source notes:
[PASTE NOTES]
```
